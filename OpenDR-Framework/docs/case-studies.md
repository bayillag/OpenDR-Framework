# Case Studies and Validation

## A. Urban Resilience (Japan)
Validation of the "fire-to-flood" risk pipeline by integrating Tokyo's **Project PLATEAU** 3D city models. 

## B. Transboundary Health (Ethiopia/South Sudan)
Application of **GRACE** groundwater anomalies and **Adaptive Otsu** surface water extraction to support malaria early warning (EPIDEMIA).

## C. Active Wildfire Watch (Regional)
Implementation of the **"Loop-in-the-Citizen"** cycle using GOES-16 thermal anomalies to provide tactical updates to field rangers.
